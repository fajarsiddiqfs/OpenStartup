🧮 What This Calculator Is

OpenStartup is a free, browser-based profit and pricing calculator designed to help small business owners, home sellers, and first-time entrepreneurs estimate their costs, selling price, and profit margin.

The calculator includes basic fields covering the most important things you need to know before starting or running a small business:

📦 Item Name 🔢 Quantity 📏 Unit 💰 Cost 📈 Profit Margin 💵 Selling Price 🏷️ Extra Costs 🏦 Starting Capital

⚠️ What This Calculator Is Not

OpenStartup is not a certified accounting tool, financial advisor, or business consultant. The results are estimates based on the numbers you enter.

You are free — and encouraged — to verify all calculations using your own methods, whether that's a physical calculator, Excel, or a professional accountant. This tool is meant to help, not replace, your own judgment.

We are not responsible for any business decisions made based on results from this calculator.
