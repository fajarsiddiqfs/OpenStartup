10 Profitable Small Business Ideas

High margin ideas with recommended pricing tiers for aspiring entrepreneurs.

Simple Profit Formula
Profit = Selling Price − Cost
Profit Margin = (Profit ÷ Selling Price) × 100
Example: Cost $3 · Selling Price $9 · Profit $6 · Margin 66%
