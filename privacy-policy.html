Privacy Policy

Simple, honest, and no-nonsense.

Last updated: March 2026

Short version: We don't collect your data. We don't store anything. Everything stays on your device. The calculator is just a tool — use it freely with zero worries.

📋 What We Collect

Nothing. OpenStartup does not collect, store, transmit, or process any personal data or information you enter into the calculator.

All calculations happen entirely in your browser. The numbers you type — your costs, quantities, prices, and product names — never leave your device and are never sent to any server.

🤝 What We Share

Nothing. We do not share, sell, or disclose any information to third parties — because we don't have any information to begin with.

We have no user accounts, no sign-up forms, and no data collection of any kind. There is nothing to share.

🍪 Cookies & Tracking

OpenStartup does not use tracking cookies, analytics scripts, or any third-party tracking tools to monitor your behaviour on this page.

If sponsored ads are displayed in the future, those third-party ad networks may use their own cookies subject to their own privacy policies — we will update this page accordingly if that changes.
